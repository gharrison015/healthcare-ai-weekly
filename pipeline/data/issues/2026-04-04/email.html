This Week's Impact
Prior Auth Reform Falls Flat at 11%
Major payers committed to prior authorization reform last summer, but AHIP and BCBSA report only 11% of requirements have been eliminated. The minimal progress suggests insurers are protecting revenue-generating denials while eliminating only low-value administrative burdens, leaving health systems to handle 89% of the prior auth workload.

Ambience Cracks Nursing AI
Ambience Healthcare launched Chart Chat, the first EHR-integrated AI copilot designed specifically for nurses, with pilots at Cleveland Clinic showing improved patient understanding and workflow confidence. Unlike standalone tools, the EHR integration eliminates context switching that typically hampers adoption in nursing workflows.

Waystar AI Hunts Revenue Takebacks
Waystar developed an AI solution to help providers recover revenue from payer 'take-backs,' where insurers claw back previously approved payments through post-payment reviews. The tool identifies patterns across claims that manual processes typically miss, addressing a revenue leakage problem that can cost health systems millions annually.
