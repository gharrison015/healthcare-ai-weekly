This Week's Impact
AI Scribes Officially Driving Up Costs
Insurers and hospitals privately agree that AI scribes are increasing healthcare costs by making documentation so easy that physicians capture more billable services and diagnoses. Nobody agrees on a solution, but expect payers to push back on higher utilization patterns these tools enable within the next year.

Prior Auth Reform Falls Flat at 11%
Major insurers have eliminated just 11% of prior authorization requirements eight months after their reform pledge, delivering minimal operational relief for health systems. The pathetic results virtually guarantee federal intervention and make AI-powered automation tools more critical for providers.

Waystar developed an AI solution to identify lost provider revenue from payer 'take-backs' - the recoupments that insurers often claw back months after payment. The tool helps health systems spot patterns and build appeals to recover hidden financial losses.
